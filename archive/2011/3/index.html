Excentric Labeling for Information Visualizationcs.umd.edu

We propose “excentric labeling”, a new dynamic technique to label a neighborhood of objects located around the cursor. This technique does not intrude into the existing interaction, it is not computationally intensive, and was easily applied to several visualization applications. A pilot study indicated a strong speed benefit for tasks that involve the rapid exploration of large numbers of objects.
