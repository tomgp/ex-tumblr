Man, Terry Pratchett, really sad news. I can’t imagine the kind of influence he must have had on me through his books — they’re not specifically life changing literature or anything but they were pretty much the go to thing for me for large a chunk of my life so really informed my sense of humour and much besides. I don’t often reread stuff but each of those discworld books I must’ve read 2 or 3 times at least and I still find them in my hand now and then.
