“There’s a reason the water faucet hasn’t changed radically over the years. Time and trial have demonstrated that the best and simplest way to control hot water is by turning a tap. Problem solved, no further solving of the hot water faucet problem is required. I cannot be the only one who is annoyed by the constantly misaligned proximity faucets in public washrooms.”

—

Steve Albini on the surprisingly sturdy state of the music industry – in full.

I have a collection on my phone of 20 or so photos of insane faucet (tap) redesigns for a future (i.e. never to be produced) photo essay about how the key skill of a designer is knowing when the solution to a problem is not in fact more designing.
