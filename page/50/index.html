“One song that never made it on the album was called “The Idiot,” and it’s about an idiot who has never been on the internet before. He goes to the post office to mail a letter and the postman says, “You idiot, don’t you know there’s emails now?” And he doesn’t know, because he hasn’t been on the internet before. At the end of the song, he gets a free trial of AOL. I always liked that tune because it has a happy ending.”

— An Oral History Of Radiohead’s ‘OK Computer’ | ClickHole (via hndrk)
