Monday, May 18, 2015

We saw Pitch Perfect 2 over the weekend. I was possibly the oldest, almost certainly the male-est person in the cinema. It was good. I liked it pretty much the same amount as first one – totally worth seeing at the cinema; that super compressed modern pop music through big cinema speakers. Though it does fall into that thing of having a character (one of the announcers) be racist and sexist etc. where we’re supposed to be laughing at the character’s cluelessness but actually the film doesn’t really do enough to make that work and you just end up with a bunch of unfortunate statements marring an otherwise fun film.

Also: Anna Kendrick is a master of looking grumpy, as a practitioner myself it’s a skill I really appreciate
